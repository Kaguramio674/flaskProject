London Business Crime Flask App

Why research on business crime?

    Business crime may lead to disastrous consequences, like significant financial losses and damages to related people. In recent years, the business crime rate has been a difficult problem for police officers. On average, there are 31.5 times of business crime happening every hour in London, how to deploy the police force effectively and rapidly becomes a large problem to police stations.
    Research on data of business in London will help officers know crime classification and distribution by area and number of a different kind of crime by month, which not only can highly improve the handling ability and efficiency to the business crime of police stations but also can lower down the crime rate.
